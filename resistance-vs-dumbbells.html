Why Resistance Bands Are Better Than Dumbbells

Resistance bands have gained popularity as a versatile, affordable alternative to dumbbells, especially among home fitness enthusiasts. Here’s a comparison of both:

Feature Resistance Bands Dumbbells
Cost Budget-friendly, often under ₹1000 Can be expensive for a full set
Portability Lightweight, travel-friendly Bulky, heavy to transport
Versatility Allows for a wide range of exercises Limited by weight increments
Space Required Minimal Requires storage space
Progressive Overload Variable resistance possible Fixed weight per dumbbell
