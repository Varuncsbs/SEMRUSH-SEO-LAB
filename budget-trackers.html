Top 5 Budget Fitness Trackers Under ₹3000

If you’re looking for budget fitness trackers under ₹3000 that deliver reliability and essential features, this curated list covers the top affordable options available in India for fitness beginners and enthusiasts alike.

1. FitTime Lite

Offers step tracking, sleep monitoring, and a week-long battery—perfect for budget-conscious users.

2. GoStep Pro

Sleek design with heart rate monitoring and smartphone notifications—all under ₹3000.

3. StepEase Band

Lightweight, durable, IP67 water resistance, and multi-sport tracking modes.

4. ActivePulse Mini

Vibrant display, calorie tracking, and excellent app syncing features.

5. TrackFit Youth

Best for beginners; tracks basic health stats and offers long standby time.

Buying Tips for Budget Fitness Trackers

  • Ensure compatibility with your smartphone
  • Prioritize battery life for less frequent charging
  • Check for warranty and after-sales support
